Problem

What the analysis needed to answer

Estimate medical insurance charges and explain which customer attributes are most associated with higher cost.

A regression workflow compares linear, Ridge, Lasso, and log-target Ridge models while checking skew, residuals, subgroup error, and coefficient direction.

Findings

The signals that mattered most

  • Charges are strongly right-skewed, so diagnostics matter.
  • Smoking status is the strongest visible cost driver.
  • Age and BMI add important context, especially with smoking status.
  • High-cost cases are harder to predict and need residual review.

  • Age, BMI, smoker, and risk-segment visual analytics
  • Statistical tests, correlation, and VIF review
